Frequently Asked Questions

When will "When It Rains" be released?

The poetry collection is set to be released in 2026. Follow on social media or subscribe to updates for announcements and pre-order information.

Where can I buy the book?

Once released, "When It Rains" will be available online and at select independent bookstores. A list of retailers will be shared closer to the launch date.

Do you do readings or events?

Yes! I love connecting with readers in person. Check my social media for upcoming events and virtual readings.

How can I get in touch?

You can reach out through the contact page for questions, collaborations, or just to say hello.

Will there be more books?

Absolutely. "When It Rains" is just the beginning. I’m always writing and have plans for future poetry collections and other creative projects.
