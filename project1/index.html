Featured Book: When It Rains

When It Rains is a moving poetry book by Sarah Parish that explores the quiet strength found in life’s darkest storms. Through heartfelt storytelling, the book follows a young woman’s journey through grief, resilience, and renewal after unexpected loss.

Perfect for fans of emotionally driven narratives, When It Rains invites readers to reflect on the beauty of healing and the power of starting over.
